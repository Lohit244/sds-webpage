Below is the Event Schedule for Day 1.

Event 1:

Bussiness Case Study Challenge

dd/mm/yyyy - dd/mm/yyyy


Round 1: Sports Analytics Case Challenge case study will be released to all the teams at a particular time. Teams will be expected to prepare their solutions to the case in a short Word document (2-pager). The top 25 teams will move to the second round.


Round 2: The 25 teams will be given a new problem state. Teams will be expected to prepare their solutions to the case as a PowerPoint presentation. The top 8 teams from this round will move to the final round.


Final Round: 8 finalists will be asked to present their case solution to a panel of judges in the final round. This round will be held online via a video conferencing software

Event 2:

Data Analytics Challenge

dd/mm/yyyy - dd/mm/yyyy



Online Assessment: Participants will get a set of 30 analytics questions which are required to be answered within 20 minutes.


Case Study Submission Submit your one or two page solution (in MS Word/Powerpoint/Excel format) to any ONE case before a particular time. The solution should include brief understanding of the problem, approach (4-5 lines), expected benefit (value), algorithm used and final evaluation metric.


Grand Finale: Shortlisted teams will present their solutions to the jury. The final dates will be announced soon.

Event 3:

Data Science Hackathon

dd/mm/yyyy - dd/mm/yyyy



The preliminary round: In this round Data Science Hackathon will consist of a total of 50 questions consisting of Linear Algebra, Probability, Machine Learning, Deep Learning, etc. Top 20 teams from the first round will be selected for the final round.


Final round: It is a 2-day long event in which each of the selected teams will have to identify a real-life problem, come up with a solution and build a working prototype of the solution. Final round is also an online event in which you will have to submit a report consisting of problem description, solution and results after implementing a solution. You also need to submit code for the solution along with instructions to run it. The solution must be implemented using Open Source technologies only. The platform for this competition will be Kaggle or Analytics Vidya Platform.

Data Visualization Quizzes:

F.A.Q

  • People from which background can participate?

    This summit is open to all and you don't need to have a specific background, although having technical, statistics /mathematics and management background may be advantageous.

  • Is it compulsory to participate in team or we can register individually?

    You can register individually as well as in team for the summit.

  • Is Data Science Summit online or offline?

    Considering the present circumstances and better outreach of the event ,Data Science Summit will be completely conducted in online mode.

  • Are there any certificates for the participants?

    Yes. Every participant will be awarded with the certificates according to the events he/she participated in.

  • How will I be judged throughout the summit?

    You will be assessed on the basis of your performance in various events by a group of experienced judges/mentors.

  • Will I we get an advisor/mentor for datathon?

    You will be provided with a mentor who will guide you during the datathon event.

  • Will I have to pay anything for attending the Webinars by experts?

    No. All the events are free of cost for you if you have registered for the Data Science Summit.

  • What if I am not a part of any college and still want to join the Summit?

    You can mail us in this scenario. You can use your last institution's ID and be a part of the Summit.

  • Can students from different colleges club together and form a team?

    Yes. Making a team of any kind is permitted. Please refer to the detailed rules and regulations on our website for further information.

  • Will I be provided with the recordings of the sessions by experts?

    Yes. If you wish, you can ask for the recordings post the session is taken. Still, it's not guaranteed due to copyright issues—we advise you to attend the event and sessions with apt focus and keep noting the information you find useful.

  • What if I am not a part of any college and still want to join the Summit?

    You can mail us in this scenario. You can use your last institution's ID and be a part of the Summit.
